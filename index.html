Why We Built This

Most people never learn to budget because it's tedious. Spreadsheets are boring. Financial apps feel like homework.

But games? Games teach through experience. They make you feel the consequences of your decisions. They keep you engaged.

Paycheck to Paycheck uses the power of games to teach financial literacy in a way that actually sticks. No lectures. No jargon. Just real decisions and real consequences.
